How to insert schedule PDF

1. Log into Omnivox
2. Open My Omnivox Services section
3. Click on Obtain my Schedule for the current semester
4. Click on Click here for printer-friendly version
5. Click on Compact printable semester schedule
6. Click on view
7. Save the PDF to your device
8. Drag and drop the PDF into the upload section
